About Us

Ortiz Fence & Welding, LLC is a family-owned business with over 25 years of experience. We specialize in custom steel fabrication and installation. Our services include wrought iron gates, rails, carports, awnings, chain-link fences, cattle fences, cedar fences, and gate operator installations.
